PaperReady is for
Documents.

An offline AI architect that builds professional PowerPoint decks and Word documents directly on your hardware.

Capabilities

COM Automation

Watches the AI interact live with Microsoft Office windows, automating the writing process in real-time.

Workspace RAG

Retrieval-Augmented Generation that searches your local folders to summarize decks and logs.

Data Sovereignty

Zero data leakage. Everything from inference to file writing happens 100% offline.
